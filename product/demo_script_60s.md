# 60-Second Demo Script — Resume ↔ JD Keyword Coverage

**Goal:** Show a job seeker paste a resume + JD, get a required-keyword
coverage score, see exactly which keywords matched and which are missing, and
act on honest suggestions — in 60 seconds. No ATS claims, no hype.

**Format:** Screen recording with voiceover. Total runtime 0:00–0:60.

---

| Time | On screen | Voiceover |
|---|---|---|
| **0:00–0:06** | A job posting side by side with a resume, keywords in the posting subtly highlighted. | "Every job posting asks for specific skills by name. Do you actually know which ones your resume says — and which it doesn't?" |
| **0:06–0:12** | Cut to the app — two clean text areas labelled **Resume** and **Job description**, an **Analyze** button. | "This is a keyword-coverage check. Paste your resume on the left, the posting on the right." |
| **0:12–0:20** | Cursor pastes synthetic sample text into both boxes (or clicks **Try a sample**). | "Everything runs in your browser. Your resume never leaves the page — no upload, no account, no server." |
| **0:20–0:26** | Click **Analyze**. Results appear instantly. | "One click…" |
| **0:26–0:35** | Score appears with its band label, e.g. **50 — Partial coverage**. | "…and you get your coverage: the share of the posting's *required* keywords your resume actually contains. Calculated, not guessed — and you can audit every point." |
| **0:35–0:44** | Lists animate in: **Required — matched** (green), **Required — missing** (red), **Nice-to-have — missing** (neutral). | "You covered React and TypeScript. Kubernetes is a required keyword your resume never mentions. Terraform? Only nice-to-have — reported separately, never in your score." |
| **0:44–0:53** | Suggestions list slides up: *"If you genuinely have Kubernetes experience, add a concrete example…"* | "The suggestions are honest, too. It never tells you to stuff keywords — only to surface real experience the posting asks about." |
| **0:53–0:57** | Badge row: **"Deterministic · Explainable · 100% in your browser."** Then the **Clear data** button clicked, everything wipes. | "Same inputs, same score, every time. And when you're done — clear it. Nothing was stored anywhere." |
| **0:57–0:60** | End card: name, tagline **"Check your keyword coverage before you apply."** + URL. | "Check your coverage before you hit apply. It's free." |

---

## Honesty rules for this video (non-negotiable)

- **Never** say or imply the tool simulates an ATS, predicts rejection, or
  explains why a past application failed. It is a keyword-coverage check.
- **Never** promise interviews or outcomes.
- **Use the real output** from the live app for the numbers on screen — do
  not fake or embellish scores.
- **Use only synthetic sample data** (the built-in sample) — never a real
  person's resume.

## Production notes

- Pace: brisk but readable; don't rush the 0:26–0:44 reveal — that's the payoff.
- Caption everything — most social viewers watch muted; matched/missing lists
  must be legible on a phone.
- No stock "AI brain" imagery. Keep it screen-real.
